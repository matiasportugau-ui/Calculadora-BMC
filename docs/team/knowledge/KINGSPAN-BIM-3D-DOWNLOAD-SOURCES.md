# Kingspan sandwich panel 3D files and BIM download sources

**Notas de uso en repo (BMC / Panelin):** documento de **referencia de investigación**; los enlaces externos pueden cambiar o requerir registro. **No** sustituye asesoría legal: la redistribución o uso comercial de BIM/texturas de terceros en `calculadora-bmc` o Shopify debe alinearse a **términos del fabricante** y a material **propio** cuando corresponda. El visor web actual consume **imágenes por URL** (Three.js `useTexture`), no `.rfa` / IFC directos; ver sección final.

---

**Kingspan provides downloadable BIM objects, CAD details, and 3D models across its own portal and at least eight third-party platforms, all free of charge.** The richest single source is NBS Source with 49 verified products and 17 downloadable Revit BIM objects. Kingspan's own configurable BIM portal at bim.kingspan.com lets users specify core type, thickness, and U-value before generating downloads. ARCAT offers the easiest no-registration access to DWG construction details for US products. Every major file format — Revit, SketchUp, IFC, DWG, STEP, ArchiCAD, and more — is covered across these platforms.

## Kingspan's own BIM portal is the primary official source

Kingspan operates a dedicated BIM configurator at **https://bim.kingspan.com/** that covers insulated roof and wall panels, architectural facades, and coldstore systems. Users select a product, configure core type (QuadCore, PIR, or K-Roc Stone Wool), panel thickness, U-value, and dimensions, then download a tailored BIM object. **Free registration is required.**

Kingspan also hosts managed **Revit .rvt system files on Autodesk BIM 360**, currently available for insulated roof and wall panels. Access requires an Autodesk account and a request to bim.assist@kingspan.com. Regional download portals provide CAD details, construction drawings, and datasheets. The GB portal at https://www.kingspan.com/gb/en/downloads/ and Ireland portal at https://www.kingspan.com/irl/en-ie/product-groups/insulated-sandwich-panel-systems/insulated-sandwich-panel-downloads both offer DWG detail libraries and link to the BIM store.

## Choosing the right source by workflow

| If you need… | Best source | Formats | Registration |
|---|---|---|---|
| Configurable BIM to exact spec | **bim.kingspan.com** | Configurable Revit | Free (required) |
| Verified UK/EU Revit families | **NBS Source** | .rfa, .rvt, IFC | Free NBS account |
| Multi-software BIM (Revit + SKP + ArchiCAD) | **BIMobject** | .rfa, .skp, ArchiCAD, IFC | Free account |
| US product DWG details, no login | **ARCAT** | .dwg, .pdf, .rfa, .skp, IFC | None |
| Render-ready formats (3ds Max, C4D, Rhino) | **Polantis** | .rfa, .3ds, .c4d, .3dm, .skp | Free account |
| Engineering CAD (STEP, IGES, SOLIDWORKS) | **3dfindit / TraceParts** | 40–150+ formats | Free account |
| Quick SketchUp models | **3D Warehouse** | .skp | None |
| Managed Revit system files | **Kingspan BIM 360** | .rvt (LOD300) | Autodesk account + email request |

## Conclusion

Kingspan's BIM ecosystem is unusually well-distributed. The official configurator at bim.kingspan.com generates specification-accurate objects but requires registration and uses a JavaScript-heavy interface. For the fastest access to professional-grade files, **NBS Source for Revit** and **ARCAT for DWG details** are the most efficient starting points — NBS for the depth of its verified product range (49 products), ARCAT for zero-friction DWG downloads. Polantis is the only platform offering Kingspan panels in visualization formats like 3ds Max, Cinema 4D, and Rhino, making it the go-to for rendering workflows. Users needing Metl-Span products (Kingspan's US subsidiary brand) should check **Modlar** at https://www.modlar.com/brands/metl-span/, which carries that range separately from the Kingspan-branded content.
